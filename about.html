About Us



Alan Smithee was a pseudonym used by directors who felt they had lost creative control of their film and did not want their names associated with the outcome. It tended to be associated with subpar films. We here at AlanSmitheePicks.com have harnessed that same ideology and compiled movies for your viewing pleasure.

We here at Alan Smithee Picks would like to welcome you to cinema hell in a handbasket. We have watched thousands of movies to find some of the worst movies ever filmed. We can't all circlejerk The Godfather can we? So grab some popcorn, turn your brain off, and sit back and enjoy some mindless entertainment.

Please Contact Us! if you have any suggestions or find a broken link. We will have a new one up and running in no time, just be sure to let us know what browser and version you were using, what the error message was, as well as what device you were on.

Thank you and enjoy...
